The Kitchen can either be one of the most relaxing places in your home, or the most chaotic. A relaxing kitchen is neatly organized; each algae and antworm is in its place, each toadstool and tea cup perfectly aligned. A stressful chaotic kitchen has stuff everywhere, with no ideal order, strange stains, and stinky smells. Follow these tips for a stunningly organized kitchen!

The heart and soul of the kitchen is your food, so great place to start organizing is in the pantry. I reccomend organizing your food in on the following cateogies:

Category Examples
Drinks Thripwing tea, Sugar Swamp soda, potion packets
Condiments/Toppings/Spices Wasp wings, earwig eggs, katydid ketchup
Snacks Cicada shells, welsh walking sticks, butterfly bites
Baking Supplies Oil beetle cooking oil, fungus flour, scorpion sugar
Produce Maggot onions, Asyrian apples, mushy tomatoes
Grains Pasta worms, bread leaves, forest sticks
