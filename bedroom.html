The Bedroom is a sacred space for rigorous relaxation. Don't allow your clutter to cloud your clear conscious! I always say that the first step to getting a good night's sleep is to have a perfectly organized Bedroom.

Consider how each of these common pieces of bedroom furniture contributes to organizing all of your awesome bedroom items.

  • The Bed
    • Are you using the space under your bed to store anything?
    • Can your bed itself be stored to provide more space inside the room?
  • The Dresser
    • How big is your dresser? Can you get a bigger one?
    • Does your dresser have enough drawers to organize each type of clothing?
  • The Desk
    • Is there anything here that you can get rid of, or might have a better home somewhere else?
    • Do you have enough folders and drawer dividers to make the most use of your desk space?
    • What are you keeping on top of your desk that might fit better somewhere else?
