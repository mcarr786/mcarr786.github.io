...foraging in the forest, collecting chrysanthemums and corriander, birch logs and butterfly wings, wondering where in the world you're going to put it all! That's why I've created this short yet staunch guide for how to turn your frenetic froggy flophouse into a space so glamorously organized, your guests will wonder if you're expecting the Frog Queen! I have advice related to organizing each room: the living room, the kitchen, and the bedroom.

Biography: Missy Mimosa is a fellow frivolous frog who enjoys thripwing tea and sewing with spider silk. Last year she danced in the French Flouncing Frog Festival, where she earned a prize for Japanese Jumping. She has won numerous awards for her Homely Home column in the Amphibian Gazette including the Best Advice Award and the Most Thoughtful Award. Ms. Mimosa currently lives in Sugar Swamp with her pet snail, Ginger.
